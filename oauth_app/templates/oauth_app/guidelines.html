Renewal Rules

  • An issued book can be renewed for a period of 15 days, subject to a maximum of 4 times.
  • A borrower can only renew/return books which are issued to them. No one is allowed to return/renew books on behalf of any other person
  • After the end semester exams, all the students must return all the books that they have borrowed from the library, otherwise an applicable fine will be imposed (please see the fine section for the details of fine).

Fine Rules

  • If a book is not renewed/returned from/to the Library within the stipulated time, an overdue charge of Rs 5/- per book per day will be charged.
  • If a book is not returned after the end semester exams, an overdue charge of 50 Rs per day per book will be incurred.
  • In case of any damage/pages missing, the borrower has to pay for entire book or the borrower can replace it with a new book of same quality
  • If a borrowed book is found to be damaged in any form (any kind of marking such as writings, underlining, or highlighting or tearing of pages) a fine of Rs 2000/- (rupees two thousand) or the actual price per book (whichever is maximum) will be imposed and the user will be suspended from the library for 15 days.
  • Stealing of books will also be considered as damaging the book and If identified, the fine will be Rs.2000/- (rupees two thousand) or the actual price per book (whichever is maximum) and the user will be suspended from the library for 15 days.
